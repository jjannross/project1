When You Do Travel

If you do decide to travel, take the time to research the best steps to take. The CDC has compiled a list of steps during travel to protect yourself and others from COVID-19:

  • Check travel restrictions before you go.
  • Get your flu shot before you travel.
  • Bring extra supplies, such as masks and hand sanitizer.
  • Wear a mask to keep your nose and mouth covered when in public settings.
  • Avoid close contact by staying at least 6 feet apart (about 2 arm lengths) from anyone who is not from your travel group.
  • Wash your hands often or use hand sanitizer.
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth.
